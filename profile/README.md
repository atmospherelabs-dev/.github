We build open-source infrastructure that makes Zcash usable — for people who want privacy without complexity, and developers who need tools to build on.

### Products

| | Product | What it does | Status |
|---|---------|--------------|--------|
| 🔐 | **[Zipher](https://github.com/atmospherelabs-dev/zipher-app)** | Shielded Zcash wallet. Warp sync, cross-chain swaps, testnet for devs. | Beta |
| 🔍 | **[CipherScan](https://cipherscan.app)** | Block explorer. Privacy scoring, mempool, in-browser memo decrypt. | Live |
| ⚡ | **[CipherPay](https://cipherpay.app)** | Private payments for merchants and AI agents. Non-custodial, no KYC. | Live |
| 🔄 | **[CipherSwap](https://cipherswap.app)** | Swap any crypto to ZEC. 15+ chains, non-custodial on-ramp. | Live |

### Principles

- **Privacy as a Default** — If it's not private by default, we don't ship it.
- **User Sovereign** — Your keys, your data, your terms.
- **Open Source** — Every line is public. We show our work.
- **Radically Accessible** — Great UX is not optional for privacy tools.
- **Invisible Complexity** — The math disappears behind design.
